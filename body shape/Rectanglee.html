Rectangle Shape

The rectangle body shape in boys and men, also known as the "straight" or "rectangular" shape, features a balanced and uniform appearance. The shoulders, waist, and hips are almost the same width.The body proportions are balanced, without any area appearing significantly larger or smaller. When you have rectangular body shape your aim should be widening the shoulders and making the lower torso appear narrow.

    Clothing for men

  • Horizontal stripes
  • Structured tailoring
  • Layered looks
  • Prints, color pops, and detailing

    Clothing to avoid

  • Double-breasted jackets
  • Baggy, shapeless clothing
  • Oversized T-shirts
  • Extremely low-rise pants
